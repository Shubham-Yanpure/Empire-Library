Empire Library Services

The Empire Library gives you access to many of the different e-Books, e-Journals and Databases, that can assist you in your studies as well as it helps you to improve your general knowledge.

1]

Reference Service

Reference service maintains a collection of reference books consists of dictionaries, encyclopedias, Audio & Video cassettes etc.

2]

Open Access

Uers can browse, read the any references in the reference section. It helps users to make full use of the resources available in the library.

3]

Book Lending

The resources borrowed from the library shall be returned before the due date, according to terms and conditions in Rules and Regulations.

4]

User Orientation

Library conducts orientation programmes for new users. It is to enable them to use library resources effectively.
